Submissions


On this section you'll see the news submissions made by your visitors.

The first option is to delete the submissions. When you click on Delete option you will NOT be promped, so please be sure about your decision.

Also you can Automate any news. Click on Auto and you will go to the Automated Story section to program the date and hour to publish the selected story.

If you want to publish the story inmediately just click on the story title, edit needed fields and send it.
