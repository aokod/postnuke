Surveys


Be cool and change your Surveys frequently :p. To add a new Poll you need to just fill in the form in this page.

Poll Title: Title for your new Poll.

Options: The poll options, please enter at least 2 options, you don't need to fill all of them in.

Delete Polls: To delete one or more existing Polls, click on the Delete Poll link. After that you will see another page with the list of all your site's polls. Check the poll you want to delete and click on the Remove button. Remember: there isn't a confirmation when you delete a poll, just click and the selected poll will go bye-bye!

NOTE: Please double check your title and your options, because you will not be able to edit the poll after you click on the Create button. This is because it is not funny to let the administrators manipulate the surveys like other systems do.
