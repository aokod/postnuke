Special Sections


Special Sections in Postnuke let you have complete articles, howto's, manuals or any big content in your site.

To add a new section just type its name in the field Section Name. Also you need to select a filename for the section graphic. The graphic needs to be located in the images/sections directory. In that directory we included a file called template.gif to show you an example of one good graphic size and format, but you can use any graphic you like.

To edit a section you need to have created at least one. Then you will see many more options. One of them is for editing a section. Click on the section name to edit it, after this you will see a new page with the option to change the the section title and/or graphic filename. From this point you can also delete the current section and of course, all of its articles!

To add a new article just type in the the article title, select the section to attach it to and then write the content and click on the Add Article button. NOTE: If you want to store the article in the database but don't want to make it visible to the visitors, just don't select the section when you add the article. When you are finished writing it simply edit the article and select the corresponding section where you want to publish it.

To edit an article, click on the Edit link at the right of the article name under the articles list box. You will see just the last XX articles added. If you want to edit older articles, just type the Article ID in the form and click on OK button. In the new page that will appear you can also delete the selected article.
